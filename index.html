NELSON MANDELA

was born in 18 of july 1918 and dead in 5 of december 2013.

leader and philanthropist who served as president of south Africa
from 1994 to 1999. He was the contry first black head of state and
the first elected in a fully respresntative democratic election. His
government focused on deismantling the legacy of apartheid by tacking
institutionalsed racism and fostering racial reconciliation.
Ideologically an african nationalist and sociolist, he served as
president of the african national congress party from 1991 to 1997
